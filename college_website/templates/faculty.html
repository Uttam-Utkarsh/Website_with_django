science faculty

Dept. of Mathematics

The Science faculty of indian College started in the year 1975 and the Department of Mathematics was established in the same year. Bachelor’s degree course in Mathematics Honours started within a few years of inception of the department along with courses in Intermediate class.

Dept. of Chemistry

The Department of Chemistry was established in 1975 with an objective of providing a platform for excellence in academic growth in Science. The Department of Chemistry is a dynamic, growing department with faculty dedicated to providing quality education. The department offers core courses in B.Sc. & M.Sc.

Dept. of Botany

In indian College, Ranchi the Department of Botany established in the year of 1975 . With the rapid advances in the field of plant science, it was felt necessary to extend it from Intermediate to Degree Level into full-fledged Department. The Department of Botany started in the Gossner Theological College building .

Dept. of Physics

Established in 1975, the Department of Physics at indian College, Ranchi, stands as a testament to decades of academic dedication and growth. Under the visionary leadership of its first head, Mr. S.C. Gupta, the department embarked on a journey to impart quality education in the realm of physics.

Dept. of Anthropology

The Department of Anthropology was established with the establishment year of Gossner College in 1971. According to the history of the college, as we heard, the college was started with 6 subjects and among them one subject was Anthropology. Reverend Dr. Nirmal Minz, the founder of the Gossner College was also an anthropologist.

Dept. of Economics

The Department of Economics is one of the constituent departments of Gossner College, Ranchi University, Ranchi. It was established on 1971 and received permanent affiliation from Ranchi University in year 1973, with its then Head S.B Mahto took the responsibility to spread the essence of economics.

Dept. of English

The Department of English was established in 1971 and it received permanent affiliation for Degree Coursewith Ranchi University in the year 1973. The department has grown from strength to strength over the years and is one of the biggest departments in the college at present.

Dept. of Geography

गोस्सनर कॉलेज, राँची की स्थापना 1 नवंम्बर 1971 ई॰ को गोस्सनर एवंजिलिकल लूथरेन चर्च छोटानागपूर एवं असम, राँची के द्वारा की गई थी। कॉलेज की स्थापना के साथ ही इतिहास विषय की पढ़ाई प्रारंभ हो गई थी। इस समय राँची विश्वविद्यालय, राँची के द्वारा इण्टरमीडिएट की पढ़ाई/परीक्षा संचालित की जाती थी। 1975 ई॰ में पहला बैच डिग्री स्तर की परीक्षा में शामिल हुआ और इसमें इतिहास विषय के विद्यार्थी भी शामिल थे।
